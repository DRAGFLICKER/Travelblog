Live and work abroad in Greece: Greece Digital Nomad Visa

Live and work abroad in Greece: Greece Digital Nomad Visa

Greece now has a digital nomad visa that will allow you to live and work remotely in the country for up to 2 years. You can even bring spouses and dependents with you.

Eligibility requirements include proof of remote work, income, and a clean background check. Check out this link for specific program requirements and check out this link for a downloadable visa application form.

Not only is Greece beautiful/historic/filled with culture, the cost of living might be lower than where you’re from. A one-bedroom apartment in Athens is only about $500-600 USD monthly. Moreover, Greece is one of the more affordable countries in Europe (excluding the touristy islands, of course).

Have you ever worked remotely or thought about it? Which country would you move to if given the chance?
